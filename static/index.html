Make a Wrapped

Turn your ListenBrainz, Last.fm, or Navidrome listening history into a shareable poster with your top artists, tracks, listening time, and favourite genre from the last 12 months.

Connect directly to your Navidrome server. Everything runs in your browser; credentials never leave this page.

Use the same credentials you log in with, or create an app-specific token. Requests are sent via the Subsonic API to your server.
